About BYU Technologies

BYU Technologies was established in 2024 in Dar es Salaam with a clear mission: to empower small and medium-sized businesses through reliable, secure, and cost-effective information technology solutions.

We specialize in delivering end-to-end IT services including custom software development, network infrastructure design, hardware maintenance, and comprehensive data backup and cybersecurity solutions.

Our approach is built on understanding our clients’ business needs, applying modern technologies, and delivering scalable systems that support long-term growth, efficiency, and security.

Our Mission

To provide innovative and dependable IT solutions that help businesses operate smarter and more securely.

Our Vision

To become a trusted technology partner for businesses across Tanzania and beyond.

Our Values

Integrity, security, innovation, and customer-focused service delivery.
